What Is a Terms and Conditions Template?

A terms and conditions template is a customizable model set of statements covering the rules and guidelines that constitute acceptable behavior when using a service. It can be used to create a section on your website where users must consent to your rules before using your service. Usually, it covers issues like copyright, liability, and privacy policy.
